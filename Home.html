Mission Statement

The mission of the Granger Police Department is to effectively and efficiently provide for the protection of lives and property, preserve the public peace, and provide needed community services with the highest level of professionalism and ethical standards.

Vision Statement

The Granger Police Department is committed to providing outstanding service with fairness, justice and equality for all in an effort to establish and maintain the highest possible quality of life for all persons in Granger, Texas. To this end, we shall interact with the community through a community policing approach to solve the problems of crime, safety and fear of crime in our community.
